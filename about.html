About us

BA Properties is a brand-new cutting-edge real-estate marketing platform, providing solutions for your residential needs. The digital site of BA Properties offers a competitive, smart and flexible alternative for listings, search, marketing and connectivity of your property requirements. Our beneficiaries include individual consumers, real-estate agents and project developers. The BA Properties team is a group of seasoned professionals in Pakistan, and abroad who have been part of multi-billion large scale digital & high-tech businesses at the global level.
The services of BA Properties are free to individual consumers for property listings. The paid packages provide project developers and real-estate agents one-stop shop with digital marketing avenues to promote their properties and projects. BA Properties is making a difference in the market with focus on a few key business performance metrics including quality of data & listing content/pictures, user authenticity, advanced site search, personalization, insights and analytics for developers and agents and a unique digitized map features to view societies on Google maps. The business model further supports customer satisfaction through dedicated account managers, and state-of-the-art call center where AARZ Consultants are available to answer any questions on smart buying, selling or renting decisions at zero cost.

Mission

BA Properties aims to deliver digital real estate solutions to the property hunters in Pakistan through better collaboration driven by technology and connectivity. Our mission is to benefit from technology to enable real estate professionals to collaborate efficiently in delivering exceptional consumer experiences.

Vision

We aspire to be the most trusted real estate business leader in Pakistan solving the traditional real estate challenges through continuous innovation and becoming a one-stop-shop for our customers.
